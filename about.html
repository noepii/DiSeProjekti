About us

We believe everyone should be in charge of their own information, and it is our mission to make our digital world safer than ever before. We are passionate about data protection and cybersecurity and want everyone to be able to navigate the internet safely. Our name, DiSe, is short for Digital Security as well.

We offer safety courses and penetration testing so you can all be at peace.

The core DiSe team is made up of Roosa, Emma, Veera, Viveka and Noe, who all founded this company as part of a UAS project.
